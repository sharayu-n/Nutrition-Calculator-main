Swasthya: Your own diet planner

Diet and health has been gaining the attention of the youth since the Covid-19 situation. People have become really particular about their calorie intakes. So, we present the ‘Diet planner’, a web based application that will track your calorie intake and help you schedule your diet daily. It will also monitor and remind you about your water intakes in case you have high sodium intake.
